Dawn of Empires

Old school RTS game on a whole new scale under development by Landell Games. Dawn of Empires will let the player choose what faction they want to play and also customize their game experience by selecting a general that drastically changes how the faction is played. The game includes large army control, but also massive War Machines and Monstrosities.

  • Check out their website here

Ninvae

Explore an unknown world full of flowers with special properties that can be used for alchemy, allowing for crafting of powerful potions. Collect flowers to fill out your herbology book and uncover remnants of a civilisation long gone. The world is procedurally generated so every player can have their own unique experience.
My responsibilites in this project included the alchemy system, sound implementation and parts of the UI elements in the game. Ninvae was nominated to Skövde Academic Game Award 2020.

  • Check it out on itch.io

Online Card Game

In Online Card Game the player takes control of three heroes and competes in a classic PvP card battle. This requires two players to play! This was a project me and two other friends started over the summer. We wanted to make an online game and figured a card game would be perfect.
My responsibilites was planning, designing and coding the UI elements in the game. The scope was set small and the game is pretty much a prototype, but I am very proud of what we were able to accomplish.

  • Check it out on itch.io

Sly Groundpound

Sly Groundpound is an endless runner that me and three friends created for the Global Game Jam 2019 in Skövde.
My responsibilites was mainly coding, updating and maintaining the UI elements of the game.
